Biography

Dr. Hui Yuan, Professor at Shandong University (promoted exceptionally), PhD advisor, IEEE Senior Member, serves on the Technical Committee of the IEEE Consumer Technology Society (Audio/Video Systems and Signal Processing, AVS), and is a member of the Technical Committee of the Asia-Pacific Signal and Information Processing Association. He is also a Senior Member of the Chinese Institute of Electronics, a member of its Youth Scientists Club, and a Senior Member and Deputy Secretary-General of the Youth Working Committee of the Chinese Society of Image and Graphics.

Dr. Yuan received his Bachelor's and Ph.D. degrees from Xidian University in June 2006 and March 2011, respectively. In April 2011, he joined Shandong University, where he has held positions as Lecturer and Postdoctoral Fellow (April 2011 - December 2014), Associate Professor (January 2015 - August 2016), and Professor (since September 2016). In August 2012, he was selected for the Hong Kong Scholar Program by the Chinese Ministry of Human Resources and Social Security, conducting research at City University of Hong Kong from January 2013 to December 2014. He was selected for Shandong University’s first “Future Scholar Program” in June 2015 and was awarded the Shandong Provincial Outstanding Youth Science Foundation in September 2016. From November 2017 to February 2018, he was a Research Fellow at City University of Hong Kong. In 2019, he was selected for the EU Marie Skłodowska-Curie Individual Fellowship, conducting research at De Montfort University in the UK and the Fraunhofer Heinrich Hertz Institute (HHI) in Germany. In December 2020, he was appointed a “Taishan Scholar” Young Expert by Shandong Province, and in August 2022, he was awarded the National Natural Science Foundation's Excellent Youth Fund. Dr. Yuan has received several prestigious awards, including the Shandong Higher Education Science and Technology First Prize (1st place, 2016), Shandong AI Science and Technology First Prize (1st place, 2020), Natural Science Award Second Prize from the Chinese Society of Image and Graphics (2nd place, 2022), and the IEEE ICME 2020 Outstanding Area Chair Award (2020). His Marie Curie Fellowship application also received the EU Horizon 2020 Seal of Excellence in 2019.

Dr. Yuan’s research focuses on data compression and analysis, particularly on the efficient compression, intelligent analysis, and reliable transmission of visual media for both human and machine consumption (high-definition video, images), as well as 3D visual media (3D video, point clouds, etc.), and robotic vision. He has published over 150 papers, with more than 80 indexed in SCI. He has submitted 50 proposals to international and domestic standardization bodies (MPEG, AVS), with five proposals adopted into the MPEG G-PCC international standard. He holds over 30 authorized patents and has led more than 30 national, provincial, and enterprise-funded research projects.
